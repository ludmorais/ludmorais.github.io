Selbetti Tecnologia

Works implemented

For privacy reasons, no specific project solution will be demonstrated, however, the work process carried out in most projects will be briefly explained, which was the most complete process I have had the opportunity to work with.
In most projects, I conducted the following complete UX and UI Design process: Briefing between UX and PO/PM; Initial Research according to the project's needs (Planning, Matrices, Qualitative, Quantitative, Benchmarking, Interviews, etc.); Interpretation and Compilation of the results of the research carried out; Presentation of the results to the interested parties; Creation of solutions; Prototyping; Usability Testing; Interoperation and Compilation of test results, Presentation of results, Prototype Adjustments; Handoff and Documentation for passing on of deliverables.
Responsibilities: Project mapping, UX research, UI prototyping, presentation of solutions, passing on of deliverables to development.
Tools and Technologies: Adobe XD, Miro, Design System, Microsoft Forms e Planner, Vegas Pro, Google Analytics, Hotjar.
