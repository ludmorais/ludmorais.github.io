Who is Ludmilla Morais?

About Me

I am a front-end developer with 3 years of experience in UX/UI design.

I graduated in Law from the University of Uberaba (Brazil) in 2022, however, I have been working in technology since 2019.

I started my technology career in Technical Support (2019). After some training, I evolved into a UX/UI Designer (2021) and now, after completing my second degree in Systems Analysis and Development from Gran Faculdade (Brazil) and after much study, I finally managed to start my long-awaited career as a Front-end Developer (2025).

Having worked in Technical Support allowed me to experience the problems faced by users. Having worked as a UX/UI Designer allowed me to be side-by-side with users, understand their pain points, and design solutions that meet their needs based on good usability and accessibility.

Today, combined with all my previous experiences, I build in code and deliver the final product directly to the users with everything they need.

As a Front-End Developer, I am pleased to deliver products to users that are built with meticulous attention to both design and code.
